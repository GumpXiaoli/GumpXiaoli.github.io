Simple Forcing Model

Discretisation in Velocity Space​

Simple Forcing scheme keeps the forcing term up to second velocity order,

Fi=wiciαcs2FαF_i = w_i \frac{c_{i\alpha}}{c_s^2}F_{\alpha}Fi​=wi​cs2​ciα​​Fα​

Its first three velocity moments are

∑iFi=0,∑iFiciα=Fα,∑iFiciαciβ=0,∑iFiciαciβciγ=cs2(Fαδβγ+Fβδαγ+Fγδαβ)\sum_i F_i = 0, \\ \sum_i F_i c_{i\alpha} = F_{\alpha}, \\ \sum_i F_i c_{i\alpha} c_{i\beta} = 0, \\ \sum_i F_i c_{i\alpha} c_{i\beta} c_{i\gamma} = c_s^2 (F_{\alpha} \delta_{\beta \gamma} + F_{\beta} \delta_{\alpha \gamma} + F_{\gamma} \delta_{\alpha \beta})i∑​Fi​=0,i∑​Fi​ciα​=Fα​,i∑​Fi​ciα​ciβ​=0,i∑​Fi​ciα​ciβ​ciγ​=cs2​(Fα​δβγ​+Fβ​δαγ​+Fγ​δαβ​)

The fourth moments are a little complicated,

Fourth Moments​

2D Case​

∑iFicix2ciy2=0\sum_i F_i c_{ix}^2 c_{iy}^2 = 0i∑​Fi​cix2​ciy2​=0

3D Case​

∑iFiciα2ciβ2=0,∑iFiciα2ciβciγ=0\sum_i F_i c_{i\alpha}^2 c_{i\beta}^2 = 0, \\ \sum_i F_i c_{i\alpha}^2 c_{i\beta} c_{i\gamma} = 0i∑​Fi​ciα2​ciβ2​=0,i∑​Fi​ciα2​ciβ​ciγ​=0
