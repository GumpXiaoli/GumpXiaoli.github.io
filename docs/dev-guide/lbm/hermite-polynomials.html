H(n)(x)=(−1)n1w(x)∇(n)w(x),w(x)=1(2π)d/2e−x2/2\mathbf{H}^{(n)}(\mathbf{x}) = (-1)^n \frac{1}{w(\mathbf{x})} \nabla^{(n)}w(\mathbf{x}), \\ w(\mathbf{x}) = \frac{1}{(2\pi)^{d/2}}e^{-\mathbf{x}^2/2}H(n)(x)=(−1)nw(x)1​∇(n)w(x),w(x)=(2π)d/21​e−x2/2

Both H(n)\mathbf{H}^{(n)}H(n) and ∇(n)\nabla^{(n)}∇(n) are tensors of rank n.
